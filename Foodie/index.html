Whether it's Amala, Iyan, Eba, Fufu, or Semo, each swallow tells a story — a story of tradition, togetherness, and the joy of sharing a hot meal around the table ...

Why Choose Amala

Amala isn’t just food — it’s a cultural icon. Made from yam flour (elubo) or plantain flour, Amala is soft, stretchy, and smooth with a unique earthy taste that pairs beautifully with rich Nigerian soups. Choosing amala is choosing culture the best food so far, weather black or white it's good
